## Plan Assumptions

- Automic Vault is treated as an open-source developer security product, not a conventional hosted SaaS.
- Current revenue, ARR, CAC, activation, retention, support, user count, and funding details were not present in the materials.
- Current plan tier is therefore treated as Tier 1: bootstrapped / pre-seed operating assumptions, with paid acquisition held until commercial motion and unit economics are defined.
- The strongest immediate leverage is owned-channel authority: homepage, docs, SEO/GEO landing pages, package pages, GitHub, founder-authored launch narrative, and developer-community distribution.
- The plan avoids recommending broad paid acquisition until there is a revenue model, activation metric, and conversion path to optimize.
